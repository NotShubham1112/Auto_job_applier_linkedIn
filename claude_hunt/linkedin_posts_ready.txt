POST #4 - LEARNING & INSIGHTS
(Monday, Week 2 @ 9am IST)

We tested pure semantic search vs hybrid RAG. Results surprised us.

Pure vector search (embedding similarity) sounds elegant in theory.

Reality: Incomplete.

On our financial intelligence platform, we A/B tested:
• Pure embedding search: High recall, lower precision
• Hybrid search (embedding + BM25 re-ranking): Better relevance, faster

Why hybrid wins:
1. BM25 catches exact matches embeddings miss
2. Combining signals improves precision significantly
3. Query expansion (HyDE) works better with hybrid
4. Real users want relevant results, not just similar

The numbers:
• User satisfaction: +45%
• False positives: -40%
• Retrieved documents: More actionable

Key insight: Don't trust research papers. Test with your actual data.

Embedding-based search is a foundation, not the complete solution.

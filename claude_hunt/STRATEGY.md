PILLAR 2: LINKEDIN AUTHORITY BUILDING
======================================

Goal: Build credible presence in AI/ML space → Get inbound interest

Strategy: Rotate 5 content pillars on 3x/week posting schedule

Pillars:
1. **Technical Breakdown** (Deep expertise)
2. **Build in Public** (Shipping energy)
3. **Systems Thinking** (Founder mindset)
4. **Learning & Insights** (Intellectual honesty)
5. **Founder Perspective** (Leadership thinking)

Posting cadence:
• Monday 9am: Technical or Learning post
• Wednesday 2pm: Build in Public or Systems Thinking
• Friday 9am: Systems Thinking or Founder Perspective

Content rules:
✅ Reference your actual projects
✅ Include specific numbers/benchmarks
✅ Show your reasoning
✅ End with genuine question
✅ Professional but natural tone

❌ Generic advice
❌ Motivational influencer energy
❌ Humblebragging
❌ Reposts without context
❌ Hot takes you don't believe

What you have:
✅ linkedin_strategy.json — Complete content strategy
✅ linkedin_posts_ready.txt — 10 ready-to-post posts (Week 1-4)

Engagement strategy:
• Daily: Reply substantively to 2-3 relevant posts in your feed
• Daily: Engage with comments on your posts
• Weekly: Follow ~10 relevant people (founders, engineers, hiring managers)
• Weekly: DM 1-2 people who engaged with your content

Goal: Build 500-1000 followers of founders + hiring managers (not vanity followers)

PILLAR 3: OUTREACH & MESSAGING
=======================================

Goal: Send 100-150 personalized emails to hiring contacts

Your advantage:
✅ Strong resume (shipped to 500+ users)
✅ Relevant projects (RAG, orchestration, SaaS)
✅ Technical depth (clear engineering thinking)
✅ Execution ability (proved by Browork + Quantexera)

Email strategy:
• Personalized for each company's tech stack
• Reference their actual product
• Show you understand their problems
• Propose conversation (not "let's discuss my resume")
• 3-4 sentences max

What you have:
✅ outreach_templates.json — Template for each company type
✅ outreach_templates.py — 12 pre-written company-specific templates

Email template structure:
1. Subject: Technical question or problem statement
2. Body: Reference their product, mention your experience, propose conversation
3. Signature: Name + GitHub link (proof of work)

Sending strategy:
• Batch send 5-10/day (don't spam)
• Spread across TIER 1 → TIER 2 → TIER 3
• 5-day gap before first follow-up
• Max 2 follow-ups per person (then move on)

Expected metrics:
• Open rate: 30-50% (personalized > cold)
• Response rate: 5-10% (quality personalization)
• Conversation rate: 2-5% (of responses that lead to actual calls)

==================================

Goal: Know your metrics, iterate on messaging

What you're tracking:
1. Outreach metrics (emails sent, response rate, conversations)
2. LinkedIn metrics (followers, post engagement, quality of engagement)
3. Response quality (which companies respond, patterns)
4. Conversion (emails → conversations → interviews)

What you have:

✅ outreach_log.csv — Track every email sent
✅ responses.csv — Log every response received
✅ linkedin_log.csv — Track post performance

Weekly review:
• How many emails sent? (Target: 20-30)
• What's the response rate? (Target: 5-10%)
• Which companies/templates work best?
• Which LinkedIn posts got engagement?
• Did any responses → conversations?

Optimization levers:
• If response rate < 3%: Personalization isn't deep enough
• If no TIER 1 responses: Maybe they're not hiring engineers
• If LinkedIn engagement low: Try different content pillar
• If responses but no conversations: Maybe email tone off

FAILURE MODES & HOW TO AVOID THEM
==================================

❌ FAILURE: Generic emails
→ SOLUTION: Each email references their product. Cut & paste signals low interest.

❌ FAILURE: Ghosting LinkedIn
→ SOLUTION: 3x/week posting is non-negotiable. Credibility builds slowly.

❌ FAILURE: Giving up after week 1
→ SOLUTION: Most responses come weeks later. Patience is competitive advantage.

❌ FAILURE: Not tracking metrics
→ SOLUTION: Use campaign_tracker.py. Can't optimize what you don't measure.

❌ FAILURE: Copying other people's content
→ SOLUTION: Authentic beats viral. Reference YOUR projects.

❌ FAILURE: Sending to every company at once
→ SOLUTION: Batch send by tier. Learning loop requires iteration time.

❌ FAILURE: Harsh tone in emails
→ SOLUTION: Friendly, curious, peer-level. You're evaluating them too.
